Welcome to my learning project Bugs, I created Bugs for educative purposes while I was learning Angular and Node.js with Express.

Often as a developer, team leader or manager we do our best to keep high levels of quality, we try to generate as less bugs as possible, but sometimes it is difficult to perceive bugs impact on our projects in real time and we might not be able to visualize the more affected areas.

Bugs is a statistics monitoring tool that help us vizualize the quality levels of our projects in real time, allowing us to take corrective measures inside our team. As we learn in real time what type of bugs are more prevalent, we can then focus in correcting the processes or affected areas.
